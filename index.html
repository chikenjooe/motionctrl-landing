FAQ

Clear answers reduce drop-off.

Is this a deepfake?

It’s motion transfer. You provide the photo and the reference clip. Only upload content you own or have rights to, and don’t impersonate real people without consent.

How long does it take?

Usually a few minutes, depending on queue and video length.

What about privacy?

For MVP we recommend deleting uploads after processing and not publishing any public gallery by default.
